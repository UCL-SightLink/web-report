About SightLink

Learn more about our project, team, and vision

Project Overview

SightLink is a COMP0016 Systems Engineering project focused on revolutionizing satellite imagery analysis through advanced computer vision technology.

Vision

To transform the way organizations process and analyze satellite imagery, making it more efficient, accurate, and accessible through cutting-edge technology.

Impact

Our solution aims to significantly reduce the time and resources required for satellite data analysis, enabling faster decision-making and insights.
